DeFi 4.0 Has Arrived

The world's first protocol for scalable, trustless and low-risk wealth management as a service, conducted in a fair, safe and transparent way - made possible through ANCAP's Delegated Farming as a Service (DFaaS) architecture.

What is Defi 4.0?

A Portfolio of Portfolios

with DeFi 4.0, the treasury is no longer entrusted to a single team of investors and farmers to manage. Instead, the protocol supports hundreds and even thousands of different fund managers appointed directly, all employing different risk strategies and managing only a portion of the treasury. The risk of ruin is dramatically reduced.

A Trustless, Unruggable Architecture

with DeFi 4.0, no individual fund controls funds directly through a hot wallet. Instead, execution of investment and farming operations is handled by a dedicated smart contract. Funds never leave the contract, and fund managers cannot embezzle or disappear with user funds.

A Powerful and Unignorable DAO

with DeFi 4.0, only authorized fund managers can interact with fund contracts, only the main DAO contract can set up and control a fund, and only the community controls the DAO contract. The community now makes the rules, not suggestions.

How Delegated Farming as a Service (DFaaS) works:

01

New wealth managers offer their services through a DAO proposal

02

$ANCAP holders vote on the proposal

03

A new, dedicated smart contract fund is created where funds are held

04

Wealth managers are authorized to interact with the new fund contract strictly to buy, sell, yield farm, etc

05

At the end of every month, profits are distributed to $ANCAP holders

06

Wealth managers who perform well can request a pay bump or a higher share of the treasury to manage from the DAO. Wealth managers who perform poorly can be removed by the DAO, and entire funds can be reassigned.
